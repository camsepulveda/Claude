# CAR T Cell Therapy Financial Analysis 2025-2026

## Executive Summary

The CAR T-cell therapy market has entered an inflection point in 2025-2026, driven by expanded indications, next-generation platforms, and dramatic expansion into autoimmune diseases. The market exceeded $5 billion in 2025 revenue with seven FDA-approved products, and is projected to reach $7.24 billion in 2026.
